Dogs by Group

More Information

Herding Group

Icelandic Sheepdog

  • Personality: Confident, cheerful, sociable; the expression “man’s best friend” was coined
    for dogs like this.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; A true herding dog: lively, inquisitive, and athletic.
  • Trainability: Eager To Please.

Old English Sheepdog

  • Personality: Adaptable, smart, gentle; OES are famous for their sweet, agreeable nature.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; A mellow housedog, but the OES is a big, agile dog
    who likes exploring, long walks, and a good gallop.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

  • Personality: Smart and alert, affectionate but not pushy, bold but kindly.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; A strong and athletic little dog, the Pembroke loves physical activity
    and is happiest when he has a job to do.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Hound Group

Basset Hound

  • Personality: Patient and low-key; a bit stubborn but always charming.
  • Energy Level: Not Very Active; Bassets are not the most active breed but do need regular
    walks to stay healthy.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Blood Hound

  • Personality: Sweet, easygoing, friendly to all; intense when working.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; a mush around the house, but relentless and stubborn on the trail.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Dachshund

  • Personality: Go look up the word “spunky.” We’ll wait. Did you see
    a photo of a Dachsie? We thought so.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; A moderately active dog, but his intelligence means
    he gets bored easily, so it’s good to keep him busy.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Non-Sporting Group

French Bulldog

  • Personality: Playful, smart, adaptable, and completely irresistible.
  • Energy Level: Not Very Active; Frenchies are easygoing, not terribly athletic;
    brisk walks will keep them trim.
  • Trainability: Responds Well.

Bulldog

  • Personality: Calm, courageous, and friendly; dignified but amusing.
  • Energy Level: Bulldogs won't beg to be exercised, but they require regular walks and
    the occasional romp.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Chow Chow

  • Personality: Serious-minded, dignified, bright, and aloof.
  • Energy Level: Not Very Active; Not particularly busy dogs, a couple of good walks a day will do.
  • Trainability: Independent.

Sporting Group

German Shorthaired Pointer

  • Personality: Friendly, smart, willing to please.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; Like most pointing breeds, GSPs thrive on running and exercise.
  • Trainability: Eager To Please.

Irish Setter

  • Personality: Outgoing, sweet-natured, active, and trainable.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; High-energy dogs who love to run,Irish Setters need
    plenty of space and exercise.
  • Trainability: Responds Well.

Sussex Spaniel

  • Personality: Merry and friendly, in the classic spaniel manner.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; Not quite as upbeat as other spaniels; walking, romping, and
    fetching will satisfy pet Sussex.
  • Trainability: Responds Well.

Terrier Group

Dandie Dinmont Terrier

  • Personality: Independent, proud, smart; affectionate at home,
    bold and tenacious in the field.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; Not a frantic terrier, Dandies are fine with a couple of
    good walks a day and some vigorous play.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Norfolk Terrier

  • Personality: Alert, fearless, fun-loving, and more
    sociable than the usual terrier.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; Brisk walks, socializing, and games of fetch will expend some
    of their boundless energy.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Norwich Terrier

  • Personality: Plucky, loyal, and affectionate.
  • Energy Level: Very Active; A tireless, alert companion, a Norwich enjoys exploring the world around him.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Toy Group

Pug

  • Personality: Even-tempered, charming, mischievous and loving.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; Pugs are not exactly natural athletes, but they do have strong legs
    and endless curiosity—exercise both regularly.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Pomeranian

  • Personality: Lively, bold, and inquisitive—a prototypical "personality breed".
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; Pomeranians are playful and vivacious but also love to be lapdogs.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Pekingese

  • Personality: Affectionate, loyal, regal in manner.
  • Energy Level: Not Very Active; Pekes do well with just a couple of walks a day and some indoor playtime.
  • Trainability: Independent.

Working Group

Great Pyreness

  • Personality: Calm, patient, smart; strong-willed yet affectionate.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; Not a particularly active dog, but these big guys need their exercise.
  • Trainability: Independent.

St. Bernard

  • Personality: Friendly, patient, outgoing; known as a great “nanny dog” for kids.
  • Energy Level: Not Very Active; A mature Saint will be content with long walks and a romp on the lawn.
  • Trainability: Responds well.

Mastiff

  • Personality: Courageous and dignified, but good-natured and docile.
  • Energy Level: Somewhat Active; An adult Mastiff is content with long walks and a good romp.
  • Trainability: Responds well.
